Front Upright
Role
Designer & Manufacturer
Skills
CAD, CAM, FEA, CNC Mill
Date
September 2023 - May 2024
Client
Northwestern Baja SAE

The front upright is responsible for connecting critical suspension and powertrain components, including wishbone arms, tie rod ends, brake caliper, and axles to the wheel hub. The 2024 Front Upright increased strength by 10% while reducing weight and cutting manufacturing times.

A significant amount of time was spent simulating various impacts, such as rollover, front collision and side collision that the front upright would experience in competition envrionments. Load cases were designed around 10,000 N of impact, with a Factor of Safety (FOS) of 1.5. Compared to the previous front upright, the new design was 10% stronger while adding negligible mass.

I wrote the CAM for the part myself. Manufacturing required two setups as custom-designed fixturing. My program cut total manufacturing time from 6 hours to 4.5 hours per part.

Reductions in unsprung mass were critical in ensuring that our vehicle handled well at our main competition, hosted by Pennsylvania Technological University, May 2024.
